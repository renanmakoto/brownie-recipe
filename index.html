Homemade Brownie

Ingredients

3 eggs

180g of melted butter

200g of sugar

450g of chocolate drops

92g of flour

20mL of vanilla flavouring essence

25g of cocoa powder

6g of salt

Prep

Step 1: Split the chocolate drops (450g) into two bowls. Each containing 225g;

Step 2: Melt the butter;

Step 3: Mix the melted butter with sugar;

Step 4: Add the eggs into the mixture. CAREFUL: Check if the butter is not too hot, otherwise it may cook the eggs while mixing;

Step 5: Add the 20mL of the vanilla flavouring essen;

Step 6: Melt 250g of chocolate drops, which you had split previously;

Step 7: Add those melted chocolate drops. CAREFUL: Again, not to cook the eggs;

Step 8: Add the 92g of flour;

Step 9: Add the 25g of cocoa powder;

Step 10: Add the 6g of salt;

Step 11: Grease a baking pan;

Step 12: Heat the oven;

Step 13: Transfer the mixture into the baking pan and bake it for 35 minutes.

Extra Info

This brownie recipe might look just like another brownie. However, it is not that common. As you see, some of the ingredients listed are not frequently found in most of the brownies recipes out there. Therefore, it is a perfect dessert.

This dish might be useful for when you are having friends over, or when you want to make a good impression.

Another important thing to point out, is that you must follow the steps thoroughly.
