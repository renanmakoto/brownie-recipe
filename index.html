Homemade Brownie

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 180g of melted butter
  • 200g of sugar
  • 450g of chocolate drops
  • 92g of flour
  • 20mL of vanilla flavouring essence
  • 25g of cocoa powder
  • 6g of salt

Prep

  1. Step 1: Split the chocolate drops (450g) into a bowl. Each containing 225g;
  2. Step 2: Melt the butter;
  3. Step 3: Mix the melted butter with the 250g of sugar;
  4. Step 4: Add the eggs into the mixture. CAREFUL: Check if the the butter is not too hot, otherwise it may cook the eggs while mixing;
  5. Step 5: Add the 20mL of the vanilla flavouring essen;
  6. Step 6: Melt 250g of chocolate drops. The ones of which you had split previously;
  7. Step 7: Add those melted chocolate drops. CAREFUL: Again, not to cook the eggs;
  8. Step 8: Add the 92g of flour;
  9. Step 9: Add the 25g of cocoa powder;
  10. Step 10: Add the 6g of salt;
  11. Step 11: Grease a baking pan;
  12. Step 12: Heat the oven;
  13. Step 13: Transfer the mixture into the baking pan and leave it baking for 35 minutes.

Extra Info

This recipe of brownie might look just like another brownie. However, it is not that common. As you see, some of the ingredients listed are not frequently found in most of the brownies recipes out there. Therefore, it is a perfect dessert.

This dish might be useful for when you are having friends over, or when you want to make a good impression.

Another important thing to point out, is that you must follow the steps thoroughly.
